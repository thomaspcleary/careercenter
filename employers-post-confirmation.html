Content Project Manager

SRA International is looking for a part-time project manager for the Education and Professional Development Micro-Accreditation Task Force.

Duties:

  • Ensure Module Development Teams remain on schedule and project goals are met. Provide status reports. Maintain a close working relationship with all relevant stakeholders including the SRAI Education and Professional Development Task Force, Module Developers, Topic Chairs and SRAI staff.
  • Design templates and terms of reference that support the Task Force goals.
  • Develop new procedures for an accredited program, including but not limited to, requirements, structure, completion criteria, evaluation and review process.
  • Copy-edit materials and liaise with content writers to ensure brand consistency.
  • Contribute to the development and presentation of a one-day training for Module Developer Chairs on developing a Body of Knowledge and course curriculum for a new accredited program.
  • Contribute to the development and presentation of a one-day training for speakers on developing online curriculum.
  • Write Case Studies for course curriculum as needed.
  • Assist in providing content for marketing materials.
  • Work with the Task Force to establish a framework for an Accreditation Review council that provides quality assurance over the micro-accreditation programs. This may include support in identifying members.

Recommended Qualifications and Experience:

  • Proven ability to manage teams to an outcome.
  • Proven experience in managing projects to deliver within specified timelines.
  • Exceptional oral, written and interpersonal communication skills with proven expertise in copy-editing and writing.
  • Background in adult learning and continuing education development a plus.
  • Extensive experience in research administration and management strongly preferred.
